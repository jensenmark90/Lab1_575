Population Change in Suburban Chicago from 1910-2010

Background

From the 1940s into the present, Chicago like most other major U.S. cities has seen the cities and towns on the outskirts explode in population! Over time, the population has continued to expand outward from the core of the city.

Use the slider bar to watch suburban Chicago grow over time and see the westward expansion that has continued into the 2010s.
